# Social Media Responsibility Checklist for College Students

## Do's
- Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- Maintain a professional online presence (future employers may check).
- Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings for your accounts.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Be respectful in comments, messages, and discussions.

 ## Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, etc.).
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
- Avoid oversharing your daily activities or location publicly.
- Do not trust or click on suspicious links or unknown messages.
